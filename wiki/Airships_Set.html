Airships Set Developer(s) Maxis Publisher(s) Electronic Arts Release date(s) June 25, 2013 Building(s) Airship Hangar, Hot Air Balloon Park, Urban Hot Air Balloon Park Cost (in USD) $8.99 Game SimCity (2013) The Airships Set is the ninth DLC available in SimCity (2013). The DLC was released on June 25, 2013 on Origin for $8.99. The DLC includes the Airship Hangar, which brings medium and high-wealth tourists into the city, as well as allowing Sims to commute via a module. The DLC also includes the Hot Air Balloon Park and the Urban Hot Air Balloon Park.
