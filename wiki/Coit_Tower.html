This article is a stub. You can help SimCity Wiki by expanding it. The Coit Tower is a landmark in SimCity 4 based on the real 1933 structure in San Francisco. Trivia Coit Tower is a 210-foot (64 m) art deco tower in Telegraph Hill in San Francisco, the contrustion was paid by Lillie Hitchcock Coit after her death in 1927. The Structure is made of reinforced concrete and designed by architects Arthur Brown, Jr. and Henry Howard. In SimCity 4, the structure has a monthly cost of 110,000 simoleons.
