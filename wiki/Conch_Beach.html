The longer beach, this site's namesake, is accompanied by a smaller, private beach that locals favor. Crude oil and raw ore deposits in this site can be used for the foundation of a specialized city. Conch Beach is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is found within Horizon Archipelago. It has a large water table, moderate ore deposits, and minimal coal, oil, and wind. It also has rail and port access. Trivia Like the other cities that share the island it sits upon, this is named after a sea creature, the Conch.
