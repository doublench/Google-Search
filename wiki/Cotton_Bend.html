Cotton Bend is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table and ore deposits, as well as moderate coal and wind resources. It has rail and port access. Trivia Given the names of the cities around it, especially Stackolee Ravine, it is likely this city is a reference to the folk song Cotton-Eyed Joe.
