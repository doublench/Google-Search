For mayors willing to clear this dense forest surrounding Connor's Ridge, coal and raw ore deposits are ripe for the picking. The riverbank is ideal for high-wealth housing or casinos. Cottonwood Forest is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has large coal and ore deposits, a large water table, and minimal wind capabilities. It has rail and port access. Trivia Given Discovery Delta's southeastern United States theme, this is likely named after the tree by the same name.
