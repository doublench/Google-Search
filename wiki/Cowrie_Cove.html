Coal deposits in this site provide a foundation for a beachside mining town. The beach running along the cove is ideal for mayors with tourism in mind. Cowrie Cove is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is found within Horizon Archipelago. It has large coal deposits, a moderate water table, and minimal wind capabilities. It also has rail and port access. Trivia Like the other cities that share the island it sits upon, this is named after a sea creature, the Cowry.
