Downloadable contents are content that are unavailable in the base game without having to download it first. In SimCity (2013), there are a total of 20 DLCs, including country-specific sets and Cities of Tomorrow pre-order bonuses. Cities of Tomorrow can also be considered a DLC, as its content is separate from the base game, however, as it has more content than that of a DLC and is also available physically, it is considered an expansion pack.
