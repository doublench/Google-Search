The most prominent pockmark on the face of this region is Fallout Epicenter, a smoldering ruin of a site. This windswept, interconnected region needs high tech recovery to unearth the natural riches buried deep within. Desolation is one of the regions introduced in Update 8 of SimCity (2013). The region consists of 7 city sites and a great work site. An Academy in the region can research the Ground Scrubber, to remove radiation from this site, providing access to the resources in this crater.
