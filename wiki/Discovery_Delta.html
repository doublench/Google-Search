Roll on down the lazy Rye River as it flows through Discovery Delta. This expansive wetland region follows the course of river tributaries with creeks and ponds forming among the city sites. Four groups of cities are situated to develop amazing cities in the delta and complete great works. Discovery Delta is one of the regions featured in SimCity (2013). It has 16 city sites and 4 great work sites.
