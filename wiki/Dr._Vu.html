Dr. Vu is the main "villain" in SimCity 4, along with Uncle Vinnie. He is a man with a medium complexion and with gray hair. He is the one who gives most of the evil missions in SimCity 4 (e.g. Disturbing the Peace, Simlent Orange, Ferry of Evil). He is also the objective in some good missions (e.g. Take Out Vu's Secret Lair, Infiltrate Vu's Secret Meeting), although he is never there. His name appears in a few buildings throughout SimCity 4; such as Vu Tank and Vat (Dirty Industry) and Vu Blue Denim Headquarters (CO$$$). Trivia Dr. Vu's name comes from producer Thomas Vu. His name could also be a reference to the first James Bond film, Dr. No.
