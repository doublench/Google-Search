Dr. Wright is a character appearing in SimCity (Super Nintendo version only). He takes the player through a  practice mode and gives them advice when they want it. He also announces disasters, rewards being available, and the city growing to a new classification. Dr. Wright acted as an advisor again in SimCity 64. His name is a reference to series creator Will Wright. In other games In The Legend of Zelda series, he appears under the names Mr. Write and Dr. Left. He's seen writing to Princess Peach and studying biology. In Super Smash Bros. Melee, he appeared as a trophy. He also appears in Super Smash Bros. Brawl as an assist trophy. His attack involves attacking people by making towers grow from below them.
