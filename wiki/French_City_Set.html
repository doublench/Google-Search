French City Set Developer(s) Maxis Publisher(s) Electronic Arts Release date(s) March 5, 2013 Building(s) Eiffel Tower, French Police Station Cost (in USD) $9.99 Game SimCity (2013) The French City Set is the fourth DLC available in SimCity (2013), released in the same day as the game itself. The DLC is available for purchase in Origin for $9.99, and is also included with the purchase of the Digital Deluxe version. The DLC includes the Eiffel Tower, which causes high wealth, medium density buildings to develop into French-style buildings. Also included in the DLC is the French Police Station.
