Harper Plains provides an entirely level site for mayors who don't want to deal with hills or rivers. This inland site has plenty of coal and crude oil for mayors to start the extraction city of their choice. Harper Plains is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has large coal deposits, but minimal oil, water, and wind. It only has rail access. Trivia Like the other cities around it, this city is a reference to one of the characters in The Adventures of Tom Sawyer - Joe Harper.
