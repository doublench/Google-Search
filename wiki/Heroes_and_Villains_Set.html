The Heroes and Villains Set is the first DLC available in SimCity (2013), available by pre-ordering the Limited Edition or by ordering the Digital Deluxe edition. The DLC includes the Maxis Manor and the Vu Tower, which is located in the police tab, as well as missions relating to MaxisMan and Dr. Vu.
