This mountaintop site has a large deposit of coal, but its true value lies in its breathtaking view of the entire region. Hiroshige Overlook is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Horizon Archipelago. It has large coal deposits, moderate water and wind capabilities, and rail access. Trivia Like the other cities that share the island it rests upon, this city is named after a Japanese artist, Utagawa Hiroshige.
