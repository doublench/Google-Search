With two tiers to build upon, this site has good amounts of natural resources. Rail running through the base of the cliff connects industry in this site to the Global Market. Hokusai Cliffs is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Horizon Archipelago. It has a large water table and wind capabilities, moderate ore and oil deposits, and minimal coal resources. It also has rail and port access. Trivia Like the other cities that share the island it rests upon, this city is named after a Japanese artist, Katsushika Hokusai.
