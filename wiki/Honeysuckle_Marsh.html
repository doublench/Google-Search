This marshy, wooded site is perfect for a mayor who enjoys the great outdoors. Both crude oil and coal within this site are ready for a clever mayor who enjoys a good causeway. Honeysuckle Marsh is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table, moderate oil and wind, and minimal coal deposits. It has rail and port access. Trivia Given Discovery Delta's southeastern United States theme, this is likely named after the plant by the same name.
