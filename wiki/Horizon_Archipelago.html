This picturesque chain of islands has several cities on each of its three major islands. These rocky islands sit high in the water, with exposed cliffs dictating interesting city planning choices. Island shores with spectacular ocean views lend themselves well to developing wealthy resorts. Horizon Archipelago is one of the regions featured in SimCity (2013). It has 11 city sites and 3 great work sites. City sites Souchong Summit: All resources but oil; Rail, Port Lapsang Landing: All resources but oil; Rail, Port Ceylon Steppes: All resources; Rail, Port Oolong Peak: All resources but oil; Rail Toyokuni Strand: All resources but coal; Rail, Port Hokusai Cliffs: All resources; Rail, Port Hiroshige Overlook: Coal, Water, Wind; Rail Conch Beach: All resources; Rail, Port Cowrie Cove: Coal, Water, Wind; Rail, Port Triton Valley: Coal, Water, Wind; Rail Nautilus Plateau: All resources but oil; Rail Trivia The first four cities are a reference to various Eastern teas. The next three are references to Japanese artists. The final four are references to shelled sea creatures.
