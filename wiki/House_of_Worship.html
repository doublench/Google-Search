The House of Worship is a ploppable building in SimCity 4 and SimCity (2013). SimCity 4 The House of Worship in SimCity 4 is the second reward building, unlocked after having 1,000 Sims in the city. The building raises the medium wealth residential demand cap, as well as raising residential desirability. SimCity (2013) The House of Worship in SimCity (2013) is a set of ploppable DLC buildings, included in Update 7.
