Hurricanes appear in SimCity 2000 and the SimCity Creator for DS. SimCity 2000 Hurricanes destroys random buildings and trigger extreme flooding. They are unstoppable. They can happen randomly if the city is located next to a coast and "no disasters" is left off. SimCity Creator (DS) In SimCity Creator (DS), hurricanes appear as rainclouds and may turn buildings into water puddles. This article is a stub. You can help SimCity Wiki by expanding it.
