A Jet fighter is a military jet equipped with bombs that you can get if you receive an Area 51. You can attack you civilians or go on missions. You earn this veichle by doing missions for Dr. Vu or entering a cheat code. The Jet Fighter is one of the most advanced vehicles in SimCity 4, so there's not much else to do than unleash your fury through bombs. This article is a stub.
