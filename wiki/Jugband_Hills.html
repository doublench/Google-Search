The Jugband Hills hold valuable raw ore deposits beneath the high plateau. Mayors who build a mining business can ship their metals out from the shoreline to the Global Market. Jugband Hills is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table and moderate coal, ore, and wind resources. It has rail and port access. Trivia This city and the ones around it are a reference to jug bands.
