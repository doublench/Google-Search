This site features a large supply of both coal and raw ore; more than enough for an ambitious smelting operation. Views of a private inlet provide an ideal location for discerning home owners. Kazoo Pass is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table and coal and ore deposits as well as moderate wind capabilities. It has rail and port access. Trivia This city and ones around it are a reference to jug bands and the instruments they play; in this case, a kazoo.
