Deposits of raw ore in this site are a tempting prize for industrious mayors. A nearby train trestle runs trains through this site to connect it to the rest of the region. Lapsang Landing is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Horizon Archipelago. It has large ore deposits, as well as moderate coal, water, and wind resources. It also has rail and port access. Trivia Like the other cities on the island it is on, this city is a reference to tea, the Chinese Lapsang souchong.
