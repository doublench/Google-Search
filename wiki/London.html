London is one of the default pre-made regions in SimCity 4. It also appears as a pre-made city in SimCity 3000 Unlimited. SimCity 3000 Unlimited London appears as a city in SimCity 3000 Unlimited. It has a base population of 304,900 people, the mayor's name is Defacto and there are 23,788 Simoleons in the city treasury.
