This site challenges mayors to curve their roads around the slough in the middle of the site. Excellent views and a nearly endless water supply make this an ideal location for the most expensive real estate. Magnolia Wetlands is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table and minimal wind capabilities. It has rail and port access. Trivia Given Discovery Delta's southeastern United States theme, this is likely named after the plant by the same name.
