The panoramic view of the archipelago from this towering site is not the only draw here. Rose Bridge helps you ship all the coal and raw ore you'll extract from underground deposits. Nautilus Plateau is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is found within Horizon Archipelago. It has large ore deposits and a large water table, as well as moderate coal and wind resources. It has only rail access. Trivia Like the other cities that share the island it sits upon, this is named after a sea creature, the Nautilus.
