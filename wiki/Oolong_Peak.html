This site rests atop a high peak. What it lacks in beachfront property it makes up for in dazzling clifftop views. A major coal deposit in the center of the peak lures mayors to invest in a mining operation. Oolong Peak is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Horizon Archipelago. It has large coal deposits, moderate water and wind resources, and minimal ore deposits. It only has rail access. Trivia Like the other cities on the island it is on, this city is a reference to tea, the Chinese Oolong.
