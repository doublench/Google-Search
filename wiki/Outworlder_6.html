Outworlder 6 is one of the disasters in SimCity (2013) and is only available with the Cities of Tomorrow expansion pack. As soon as the Outworlder 6 arrives in the city, it will begin to desperately absorb power and ControlNet in order to fuel its escape. This leaves the city in darkness and without a large amount of ControlNet for some time unless the player have a very large power or ControlNet grid. This article is a stub.
