For the Twin Towers in New York, see World Trade Center. The Petronas Twin Towers are landmark buildings available in SimCity 3000. Building info The building was built in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The twin towers is 451 meters tall and joined by a skybridge and a shopping center, and was built in the 1990s. By the time it was completed in 1996, it was the world's tallest building, just behind the CN Tower, Toronto in terms of the tallest buildings on Earth. The record was broken by Taipei's 101 Tower a few years later, but the Towers are still the world's tallest twin structures until today. In the games Even though the building has a twin structure, the towers are built separately like the former New York World Trade Center. This meant the towers in game has no skybridges. The towers are one of two structures from Kuala Lumpur to be used in game, the other being the Maybank Tower (as a commercial skyscraper in Unlimited).
