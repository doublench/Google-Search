Progressive Insurance Set Developer(s) Maxis Publisher(s) Electronic Arts Release date(s) November 6, 2013 Building(s) Progressive Office Cost (in USD) Free (no longer available) Game SimCity (2013) The Progressive Insurance Set is the twelfth DLC available in SimCity (2013), released for free on Origin on November 6, 2013. The DLC adds building insurance to the game and comes in the form of a progressive insurance office building. The DLC includes the Progressive Office, which provides building insurance to the city, as well as providing a place for shoppers. The DLC also includes two missions based on the Progressive Insurance mascot, Flo. The DLC is an another example of corporate sponsorship in SimCity (2013), the first being the Nissan Leaf Charging Station. Progressive is also a real company, based in the United States. As of July 31, 2014, the Progressive Insurance Set is no longer available to download from the Origin store.
