Railroads are transport tiles used in SimCity (SNES) In Simcity (SNES) Railroads in Simcity SNES function almost identically to railroads in the SimCity editions on other platforms. They transport sims between the games zones, without the player having to worry about traffic congestion present on Roads. Railroads can be placed on land tiles and water tiles as a Subway; however when placing in water the railroad is unable to place corners, these can only occur on land tiles. Sims cannot join a rail line that is underwater, they can only transit through it. A single railcar can be spotted on the city's rail network, however this has no game effect. Special Buildings Ater placing a certain number of Railroad titles, the player has the option of building a central station in their city. Gallery
