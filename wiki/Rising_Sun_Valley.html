Rising Sun Valley is ideal for mayors who long for wide open spaces. A modest supply of crude oil and coal in this site welcomes mayors to start a drilling business or provide a fine source of coal for a power plant. Rising Sun Valley is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table, moderate coal and oil reserves, and minimal wind capabilities. It has rail access only. Trivia Given the names of the cities around it, especially Stackolee Ravine, it is likely this city is a reference to the folk song The House of the Rising Sun.
