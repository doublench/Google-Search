San Francisco is a major real-life city located in the US state of Cailfornia. In the first and second game of the SimCity series, San Fransisco is rocked by a massive earthquake as part of a scenario. This earthquake sparks massive fires that burn through the city until there is nothing left. In SimCity and SimCity 2000 they are based on real events. In SimCity 4 it is a region. SimCity In SimCity, San Francisco is shaken by the massive 1906 San Francisco earthquake. The player must contain the fires and rebuild from the rubble and damage which is left from the earthquake. SimCity 2000 In SimCity 2000, San Francisco is shaken by the 1989 Loma Prieta earthquake. The player must contain the fires and rebuild the city. SimCity 4 In SimCity 4, San Francisco is instead a pre-made region based on a terrain map where the player can build on and develop buildings and life on. A wide river runs throughout the region. The scale of the map is not accurate, so the player would not be able to reach the population that is here in real life which is roughly 800,000 people. The city of San Francisco is also divided into 4 medium sized maps instead of all one large map.
