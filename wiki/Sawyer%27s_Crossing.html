Sawyer's Crossing is ready for adventurous mayors willing to cross Sawyer Creek, out to the island. Coal and raw ore deposits provide mayors with a reliable income. Sawyer's Crossing is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table, moderate ore deposits, and minimal coal and wind. It has rail and port access. Trivia Like the other cities around it, this city is a reference to one of the characters from The Adventures of Tom Sawyer - Tom Sawyer.
