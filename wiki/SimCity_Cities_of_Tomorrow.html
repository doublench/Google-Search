SimCity: Cities of Tomorrow Developer(s) Maxis Publisher(s) Electronic Arts Release date(s) November 12, 2013 November 14, 2013 November 14, 2013 November 15, 2013 Mode(s) Single-player, multiplayer Platform(s) Microsoft Windows, OS X Media Digital download Input methods Mouse, keyboard Ratings ESRB: E10+ PEGI: 7 Series SimCity SimCity: Cities of Tomorrow is an expansion pack released for SimCity (2013). The expansion includes several new futuristic devices from 50 to 75 years in the future and was released on November 12, 2013. The expansion is fully compatible with vanilla SimCity players. Features Flying drones that can make deliveries and serve as policemen and firemen and doctors. A new resource called Omega. It infects buildings and vastly increases their money output but severely increase the pollution they emit as well. Customizable MegaTowers and Elite MegaTowers for the wealthy. (Skybridges for connecting them) The Academy New resource "ControlNet" Garbage Atomizers that completely destroy waste. Wave Power Generators that generate electricity from the ocean. Ground Scrubbers that turn ground pollution and radiation into air pollution that can then be blow away.
