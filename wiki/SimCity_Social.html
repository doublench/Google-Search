SimCity Social is a 2012 online SimCity spin-off game for Facebook. The game is free to play, but uses the freemium model, where the player must pay to unlock other features. The game is now closed and unavailable to play It runs like normal SimCity titles, however the player can only build when their energy is high. The player can either get it by just letting time pass, or by completing missions. Shutdown On Monday, April 22nd, 2013, EA announced that SimCity Social, alongside The Sims Social, will both be shut down permanently on June 14th, 2013, with no chance of returning. This marks the 4th EA Sim series title to shut down.
