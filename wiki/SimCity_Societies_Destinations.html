SimCity Societies: Destinations Developer(s) Tilted Mill Entertainment Publisher(s) Electronic Arts Release date(s) June 23, 2008 Genre(s) City building simulation Mode(s) Single-player Platform(s) PC Input methods Mouse, keyboard Ratings ESRB: E10+ Series SimCity SimCity Societies: Destinations is an expansion pack for SimCity Societies, released on June 23rd, 2008. The expansion pack is also included in SimCity Societies: Deluxe Edition and The SimCity Box. Game Summary SimCity Societies: Destinations is about creating a vacation resort city. The player can raise their city's fame up to five stars by adding more hotels and resorts.
