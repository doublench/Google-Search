The SimCity series is a video game series dedicated to open-ended city-building and urban simulation. It was created by Will Wright's Maxis and published in 1989. The series continues under the publisher Electronic Arts and developer Maxis. SimCity has had unprecedented success and is considered by many to be the founder of the modern city-builder genre among PC games, and is also considered to be the inspiration for many other games, such as Tropico and Cities XL, as well as the Facebook game, Cityville.
