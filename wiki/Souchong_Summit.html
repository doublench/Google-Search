Coal deposits in this site provide a great starting point for mayors willing to work at high altitudes. Rail running up the mountainside provide it another welcome connection to the region. Souchong Summit is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Horizon Archipelago. It has large coal deposits, moderate water and wind resources, and minimal ore deposits. It has rail and port access. Trivia Like the other cities on the island it is on, this city is a reference to tea, the Chinese Lapsang souchong.
