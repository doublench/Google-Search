Mayors yearning to become an oil baron will find a motherload of crude oil that will do plenty more than run your Oil Power Plant. River access for shipping to the Global Market provides necessary infrastructure. Spooner Plains is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table oil deposits as well as moderate wind capabilities and minimal coal reserves. It only has port access. Trivia This city and the ones around it are a reference to jug bands and the instruments they play; in this case, the spoons.
