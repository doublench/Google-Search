The creeks running through Stackolee Ravine offer a challenge to mayors. Mayors who rise to this challenge can expect to find coal or crude oil deposits as they navigate the five sections of this site. Stackolee Ravine is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table and coal deposits, moderate wind capabilities, and minimal oil reserves. It has rail and port access. Trivia Given the names of the cities around it and its own unusual name, it is likely this city is a reference to the folk song Stagger Lee. Stackolee is one of the variations of its name.
