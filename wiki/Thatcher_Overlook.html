The upper portion of this site has a blend of coal and raw ore deposits to give mayors balanced resources for a smelting business. The shoreline in the lower area is ideal for trading. Thatcher Overlook is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has large coal and water resources and minimal ore and wind. It has rail and port access. Trivia Like the other nearby cities, this city is a reference to possibly two characters in The Adventures of Tom Sawyer - Becky Thatcher and/or Judge Thatcher.
