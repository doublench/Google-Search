The SimCity Box Developer(s) Maxis, Tilted Mill Entertainment Publisher(s) Electronic Arts Release date(s) June 23, 2008 Genre(s) City building simulation Platform(s) PC Input methods Mouse, keyboard Ratings ESRB: E10+ Series SimCity The SimCity Box is a compilation that includes SimCity 4 and its expansion pack Rush Hour, SimCity Societies and its expansion pack Destinations, as well as the spinoff The Sims Carnival: SnapCity.
