The shoreline that sweeps around the edge of this site provides highly desirable land to build upon. Massive crude oil and raw ore deposits force mayors to decide between business and pleasure. Toyokuni Strand is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Horizon Archipelago. It has a large water table and ore and oil deposits, but only minimal wind capabilities. It also has rail and port access. Trivia Like the other cities that share the island it rests upon, this city is named after a Japanese artist, Utagawa Toyokuni.
