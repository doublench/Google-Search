Coal in this site is found throughout three tiers of elevation, allowing for tunnels, ramps, and highly desirable cliffs. Rail connects to Nautilus Plateau on its way to the record-setting Rose Bridge. Triton Valley is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is found within Horizon Archipelago. It has large coal deposits and wind capabilities, a moderate water table, and rail access. Trivia Like the other cities that share the island it sits upon, this is named after a sea creature, the Triton.
