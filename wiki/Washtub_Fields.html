The Washtub Fields provide plenty of space for mayors to expand. Massive crude oil deposits allow mayors to grow their city into an oil-driven metropolis. River access in this site is ideal for shipping resources. Washtub Fields is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has a large water table and oil reserves, as well as moderate wind capabilities and minimal coal deposits. It has rail and port access. Trivia This city and the ones around it are a reference to jug bands and the instruments they play; in this case, a washtub bass.
