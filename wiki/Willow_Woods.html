Deep within this huge forest is a deep vein of raw ore and a modest supply of coal. Mayors can build a massive city within the trees of this heavily forested site. Willow Woods is one of the cities featured in SimCity (2013). It is located within Discovery Delta. It has large ore and water resources, as well as moderate coal and wind. It has rail and port access. Trivia Given Discovery Delta's southeastern United States theme, this is likely named after the tree by the same name.
