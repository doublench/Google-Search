This article is a stub. You can help SimCity Wiki by expanding it. A zombie attack is a disaster that occurs in SimCity (2013). It is the second rarest disaster and can be helped by deploying the police to try and get them down. Effects on City When a zombie attack occurs or triggered, it will play a disaster music different from the music used by a few other disasters. Visible zombies walk on streets like normal pedestrians, and have a green glowing body. They will devour residents of your city, leaving them abandoned. This can have a negative effect on your city in you want high population. The disaster will go on for the rest of the game night until sunrise occurs.
